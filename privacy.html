Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dreamoir ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Data Type Purpose Stored Where

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number.
  • We do not collect your location data.
  • We do not use advertising trackers or analytics SDKs.
  • We do not sell, share, or rent your personal data to third parties.

3. Anonymous Authentication

Dreamoir uses anonymous authentication based on your device's vendor identifier (a unique ID assigned by Apple to your device for this app). No personal credentials such as email or password are required. This means:

  • Your account is tied to your device, not to your personal identity.
  • If you uninstall and reinstall the App, a new anonymous account may be created.
  • Your subscription is linked via your Apple ID transaction, ensuring cross-device continuity for purchases.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide the App's functionality:

  • Supabase — Database, authentication, file storage, and serverless functions. Data is stored on Supabase's cloud infrastructure. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Replicate — AI image and sticker generation. Your dream text prompts (not personal data) are sent to Replicate's API for processing. Replicate Privacy Policy
  • DeepSeek — AI dream interpretation and prompt enhancement. Your dream text is sent to DeepSeek's API for processing. DeepSeek Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit — In-app purchases and subscription management. All payment processing is handled by Apple. Apple Privacy Policy

5. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI dream images, stickers, and interpretations based on your input.
  • To store and display your generated content in the App's gallery.
  • To manage your subscription status and enforce generation limits.
  • To process and verify in-app purchases.

6. Data Retention

  • Generated images and dream data are stored as long as your account exists.
  • We may implement automatic cleanup of old data to manage storage costs. If so, users will be notified in advance.
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmission between the App and our servers uses HTTPS encryption.
  • Database access is restricted via Row Level Security (RLS) policies — users can only access their own data.
  • API keys for third-party services are stored securely in server-side environment variables, not in the App.

8. Children's Privacy

Dreamoir is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with information, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.
